# Tone Variation with the Same Task

## Prompt 1: Write an email inviting a candidate to an interview in a friendly tone

Hi [Candidate Name],

We loved reviewing your application and would be thrilled to invite you for an interview with our team!

Here are the details:
Date & Time: [Insert date & time]
Location/Format: [Insert location or video call link]
Interviewer: [Name & Role]

Please let us know if this time works for you or if we should adjust. We’re looking forward to getting to know you better and chatting about the role!

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Your Job Title]
[Company Name]

## Prompt 2: Write an email inviting a candidate to an interview in a professional tone

Dear [Candidate Name],

Thank you for applying for the [Job Title] position at [Company Name]. We were impressed with your background and would like to invite you to an interview.

Details:

Date & Time: [Insert date & time]

Location/Format: [Insert location or video call link]

Interviewer: [Name & Role]

Please confirm your availability for the scheduled time. If you require an alternative time, let us know, and we will do our best to accommodate you.

We look forward to speaking with you.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Your Job Title]
[Company Name]
[Contact Information]

## Compare the outputs and note how the tone changes while the content stays consistent.

Yes, the content was consistent but the tone changed as requested.
