# Instruction Refinement

## Start with a vague request like: “Tell me about AI.”

I ran this prompt on several AI models many times and the structure of the output are different. There's room of assumptions with this prompt where AI has no knowledge of my experience and might answer like it's too a 5-year old or to an AI researcher

### Refine it step by step into a clear instruction, e.g.,

#### Explain AI in simple terms for beginners.

This provided the level of experience of me, the reader, and the AI is now able to explain in this regards. This is better than the original prompt because it provided a contraint on how the AI should answer me. This is a refinement of audience.

#### Summarize the main AI techniques in 5 bullet points.

This enforced AI to answer in a particular structure, list five bullet points. Rather than have AI randomness in the structure of response, this enforces a contrainst for structure. This is a refinement of format.

##### Explain AI with mathematical foundations.

This provides a refinement to how much I need to know about AI or to what depth do I need to know. This is a refinement of depth

#### Explain how AI is used in healthcare.

This is a refinement of focus area. It aims to allow AI explain the prompt to me in regards to particular perspective or focus area.
