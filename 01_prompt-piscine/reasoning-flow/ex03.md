# Multi-Step Question Answering

## Selected multi-step question

What is 25% of the sum of 40 and the product of 10 and 6?

## Prompts

### Restate the problem:

**Prompt:** Restate the problem of "What is 25% of the sum of 40 and the product of 10 and 6?"

**AI's response:** What is one quarter of the total you get when you add 40 to the result of multiplying 10 by 6?

### Break it into smaller steps

**Prompt:** Break the problem, "What is one quarter of the total you get when you add 40 to the result of multiplying 10 by 6?" into smaller steps
**AI's response:**

- Multiply 10 by 6.

- Add 40 to that result.

- Find one quarter of the total you get.

### Solve each step before giving the final answer

**Prompt:** Solve each step before giving the final answer

**AI's response:**

Multiply 10 by 6.
10 × 6 = 60

Add 40 to that result.
60 + 40 = 100

Find one quarter of the total.
100 ÷ 4 = 25

Final answer: 25

## Review

The AI was correct in all of it's steps
