## Factual Question Prompt with Instructions

`What is the famous speech made by Martin Luther King Jr. Cite the source in your answer and list the source(s) using the APA style`

### Output

The most famous speech made by Martin Luther King Jr. is “I Have a Dream.” He delivered it on August 28, 1963, at the March on Washington for Jobs and Freedom in Washington, D.C. during the American civil rights movement. In the speech he called for an end to racism and for equality of civil and economic rights in the United States.

A widely quoted excerpt from the speech reads:

“I have a dream that one day this nation will rise up and live out the true meaning of its creed … that all men are created equal.”
