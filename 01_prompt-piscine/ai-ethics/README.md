## Summary on AI and self-reliance

AI is a powerful tool, but it shouldn't replace in-depth thinking as a software engineer. Self-reliance means understanding why things work and knowing when to make tradeoffs.

## Insight about using AI ethically as a coder.

- Intent: Use AI to learn and understandand. Not to thoughlessly copy-and-paste
- Awareness: Be aware of the limit of AI. Generated code can be flawed and AI can make mistakes

## Plan to apply these principles in future quests.

In future, I plan to

- think first, prompt later
- seek to understand rather than finding quick answers
- review everything critically
