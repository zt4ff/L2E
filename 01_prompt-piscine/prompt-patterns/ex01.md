# Data Extraction Template

## Weak Prompt

`Summarize this text.`

### Input 1 – News Article

"On Monday, the government announced a new renewable energy policy aimed at reducing carbon emissions by 40% before 2035. The plan includes subsidies for solar installations and penalties for high industrial polluters. Officials argue the policy will create jobs and stimulate innovation."

### Output (Weak Prompt)

The government announced a renewable energy policy to reduce emissions. It includes subsidies and penalties. Officials believe it will help the economy.

## Refined Prompt Pattern

`Summarize the following text in 3 bullet points, focusing on the main arguments and ignoring examples.`

### Input 1 – News Article

"On Monday, the government announced a new renewable energy policy aimed at reducing carbon emissions by 40% before 2035. The plan includes subsidies for solar installations and penalties for high industrial polluters. Officials argue the policy will create jobs and stimulate innovation."

### Output (Refined Prompt)

- Government introduced a renewable energy policy targeting a 40% emission reduction by 2035.

- The policy uses financial incentives and penalties to influence industry behavior.

- Leaders claim it will drive job creation and innovation.

### Input 2 – Technical Blog

"Microservices architecture allows applications to be broken into smaller independent services. Each service can be deployed separately, improving scalability. However, it increases system complexity and requires strong monitoring tools."

### Output (Refined Prompt)

- Microservices divide applications into smaller independent services.

- This approach improves scalability and independent deployment.

- It introduces added system complexity and monitoring requirements.

## Reflection

The refined prompt enforces structure and focus. Outputs are consistent and avoid unnecessary detail. Bullet format improves clarity.
